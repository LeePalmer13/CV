About Me

After a 2 year course I had earned my City and Guilds level two and was sent, after a further brief vehicle equipment course, to Germany. After leaving the Army I held various roles throughout several local garages where due to poor health I was forced to re-skill.

I have studied with The Open University for a little over 10 years and earned an M.A. in Classical Studies. The experience I have developed academically has between extremely valuable in both academic writing and research. Searching for further challenges I am studying Computer Science and Web Development. Although still new to the field I believe that I can make a valuable contribution to someone's web prescence through the use of my coding skills.
